# Output Contracts

## 1. Hidden Risk Summary

### Required sections

- scope reviewed
- top hidden risks
- likely impact areas
- assumptions
- unresolved questions
- source anchors

## 2. Complexity Heat Map

### Required sections

- complexity area
- why it matters
- severity
- confidence
- source anchors

## 3. Decision Brief

### Required sections

- decision under review
- options considered
- evidence summary
- inferred concerns
- top risks
- recommended next checks
- unresolved questions

## 4. Pattern Remix Draft

### Required sections

- target outcome
- reusable prior patterns
- constraints
- proposed approach
- implementation steps
- known risks

## 5. Context Packet

### Required sections

- source name
- section or scope reviewed
- extracted findings
- caveats
- confidence or ambiguity notes
- source anchors
